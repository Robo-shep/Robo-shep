## 🚀 About Me

I’m an AI/ML student working toward becoming a systems-level machine learning engineer.

Rather than relying purely on frameworks, I focus on:
- Understanding model internals  
- Implementing algorithms from first principles  
- Connecting mathematical theory with real-world systems  

My interests include:
- Neural networks and representation learning  
- Reinforcement learning  
- Intelligent recommendation systems  
- Applied research in AI  

I approach development with a bias toward structure, clarity, and long-term thinking.  
The goal is not just to build models — but to understand, optimize, and deploy them effectively.
